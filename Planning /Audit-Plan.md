## Engagement Objective
Assess whether logical access controls are designed appropriately and operating effectively to prevent unauthorized access.

## Risk Considerations
- Excessive user privileges
- Lack of periodic access reviews
- Inadequate evidence retention
- Delayed removal of terminated users

## Control Areas
- User provisioning and deprovisioning
- Periodic access certification
- Privileged access management
- Evidence retention

## Approach
- Review policies and procedures
- Perform sample-based testing
- Validate evidence completeness
- Document results and conclusions
