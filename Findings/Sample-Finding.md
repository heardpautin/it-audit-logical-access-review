# Audit Finding – Logical Access Review Gap

## Condition
Periodic access reviews were not consistently documented for a subset of users.

## Criteria
Organizational access control policy requires documented access certification on a defined schedule.

## Cause
Incomplete tracking of access review completion.

## Risk
Unauthorized or excessive access may persist without detection, increasing security and compliance risk.

## Recommendation
Implement standardized access review tracking and require documented sign-off for all reviews.

## Management Response
Management agrees and will implement improved tracking and documentation procedures.

## Status
Open – remediation in progress
